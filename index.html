Urban Landscapes

An Experiment by Nikolaus Stachnik

Nikolaus Stachnik is an Designer from Berlin.

The motifs of "Urban Landscapes" are inspired by our environment, which is not perceived by us. We just walk by...

"Urban Landscapes" deals with shape color and context of metropolitan cities construction sites.

Neither ugliness nor beauty were the criteria according to which the subjects were selected.
